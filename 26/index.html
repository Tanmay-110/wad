PICT B.E. Course Structure

First Year (FE)

  • Engineering Mathematics I & II: Foundation in calculus, linear algebra, and differential equations.
  • Applied Physics: Mechanics, optics, and modern physics concepts.
  • Applied Chemistry: Chemical principles relevant to engineering materials.
  • Basic Electrical Engineering: Circuits, DC/AC analysis, and electrical machines.
  • Engineering Mechanics: Statics and dynamics of particles and rigid bodies.
  • Workshop Practice: Hands-on skills in carpentry, welding, and machining.
  • Environmental Studies: Basics of environmental science and sustainability.

Second Year (SE)

  • Data Structures & Algorithms: Lists, trees, graphs, and algorithmic analysis.
  • Discrete Mathematics: Logic, set theory, and combinatorics.
  • Digital Electronics: Logic gates, combinational and sequential circuits.
  • Object Oriented Programming: Classes, inheritance, and polymorphism using C++/Java.
  • Computer Organization: CPU architecture, memory, and I/O systems.
  • Operating Systems Basics: Processes, threads, and scheduling.

Third Year (TE)

  • Database Management Systems: ER models, SQL, and normalization.
  • Computer Networks: OSI model, TCP/IP, and network protocols.
  • Software Engineering: SDLC, requirement analysis, and design patterns.
  • Microprocessors: Architecture and interfacing of 8086/ARM processors.
  • Theory of Computation: Automata, decidability, and complexity classes.
  • Web Technologies: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and client-server models.

Fourth Year (BE)

  • Machine Learning: Supervised and unsupervised learning algorithms.
  • Cloud Computing: Virtualization, AWS/Azure fundamentals.
  • Mobile App Development: Android/iOS principles and frameworks.
  • Internet of Things: Sensors, connectivity, and IoT architectures.
  • Capstone Project: End-to-end engineering project under faculty guidance.
  • Electives: Choose from cybersecurity, data analytics, AI, etc.
